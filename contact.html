Frequently Asked Questions

Appointment Booking

You can book an appointment through our online booking system by visiting the "Book Appointment" page. Simply fill out your personal information, select the service you need, choose a clinic location and time, then confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation email within 24 hours.

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment by calling our support line at +254 700 000 000 or using the live chat feature. Please provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations to allow other patients to use the time slot.

Please bring: valid identification, any previous medical records, a list of current medications, and insurance information if applicable. For child appointments, bring the child's vaccination card if available.

Mobile Clinic Services

Mobile clinics provide a range of services including general checkups, vaccinations, maternal care, child health services, emergency care, mental health support, and nutrition counseling. Specific services vary by clinic and location.

Mobile clinic schedules vary by location and community needs. Some areas receive weekly visits, while others may have monthly or bi-monthly schedules. Check our clinic locations page for specific schedules in your area.

Many basic health services are provided free of charge or at subsidized rates. Some specialized services may have minimal fees. Payment options include cash, mobile money, and health insurance where applicable.

Locations & Coverage

Community leaders or health coordinators can request mobile clinic services by filling out our contact form with "Request Mobile Clinic" as the inquiry type, or by calling our support line. We evaluate requests based on community need, accessibility, and resource availability.

We currently serve rural and underserved communities across Kenya, with active clinics in Nairobi, Kiambu, Turkana, Narok, and Kisumu counties. We're continuously expanding our coverage to reach more communities in need.

Technical Support

Our technical support team is available through live chat, phone support, or email. We can help with website navigation, booking issues, or any other technical difficulties you may encounter.

Currently, our services are available through this responsive website that works well on mobile devices. A dedicated mobile app is in development and will be available soon.

Our Mission

HealthConnect was born from a simple yet powerful vision: every person, regardless of their geographic location, deserves access to quality healthcare.

In East Africa, millions of people in rural and remote communities face significant barriers to accessing basic healthcare services. Long distances to health facilities, irregular clinic schedules, and limited health information create gaps that can be life-threatening.

Our Journey

2024 Q1

Concept & Research

Identified healthcare accessibility challenges in rural East Africa and began developing technology solutions.

2024 Q2

Platform Development

Built the core HealthConnect platform with mobile clinic tracking, appointment booking, and health resources.

Built with Modern Technology

HealthConnect leverages cutting-edge web technologies to deliver a fast, reliable, and accessible healthcare platform that works across all devices and internet conditions.

Mobile-First Design

Optimized for smartphones and tablets, ensuring accessibility in areas where mobile devices are primary internet access points.

Interactive Mapping

Real-time clinic location tracking with Leaflet.js and OpenStreetMap integration for accurate navigation.

Offline Capability

Progressive Web App features allow core functionality even with limited internet connectivity.

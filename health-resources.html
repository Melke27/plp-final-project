Disease Prevention

Malaria Prevention

Learn how to protect yourself and your family from malaria through proper use of mosquito nets, repellents, and environmental management.

  • Sleep under insecticide-treated nets every night
  • Remove standing water around your home
  • Use mosquito repellent during peak hours (dusk and dawn)
  • Wear long-sleeved clothing when outdoors
Prevention Mosquito Control

Water Sanitation & Hygiene

Safe water practices and proper hygiene are essential for preventing waterborne diseases like cholera and typhoid.

  • Boil water for at least 1 minute before drinking
  • Wash hands with soap for 20 seconds regularly
  • Use proper sanitation facilities
  • Store food in clean, covered containers
Hygiene Water Safety

Maternal Health

Prenatal Care Essentials

Important steps for maintaining health during pregnancy and ensuring safe delivery.

  • Attend regular prenatal checkups
  • Take folic acid and iron supplements
  • Eat nutritious foods rich in vitamins
  • Avoid alcohol, tobacco, and harmful substances
  • Get adequate rest and moderate exercise
Pregnancy Nutrition

Warning Signs During Pregnancy

Know when to seek immediate medical attention during pregnancy.

  • Severe headaches or vision changes
  • Heavy bleeding or fluid leakage
  • Severe abdominal pain
  • Decreased fetal movement
  • High fever or persistent vomiting

Recognizing Childhood Illnesses

Common signs and symptoms that require medical attention in children.

  • High fever (over 38°C/100.4°F)
  • Difficulty breathing or rapid breathing
  • Persistent diarrhea or vomiting
  • Unusual drowsiness or irritability
  • Skin rashes or unusual discoloration

Emergency Care

Basic First Aid

Essential first aid skills that could save a life in emergency situations.

1. Check for Responsiveness: Tap shoulders and shout "Are you okay?"
2. Call for Help: Contact emergency services immediately
3. Check Breathing: Look, listen, and feel for normal breathing
4. Control Bleeding: Apply direct pressure with clean cloth
First Aid Emergency

When to Seek Emergency Care

Critical signs that require immediate medical attention.

  • Chest pain or difficulty breathing
  • Severe bleeding that won't stop
  • Loss of consciousness or confusion
  • Severe burns or injuries
  • Signs of stroke (facial drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty)

Nutrition & Wellness

Balanced Diet Essentials

Key components of a healthy diet for optimal health and disease prevention.

  • Include fruits and vegetables in every meal
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains
  • Include protein sources like beans, fish, and lean meat
  • Limit processed foods and sugary drinks
  • Drink plenty of clean, safe water daily
Nutrition Prevention

Managing Diabetes

Essential information for managing diabetes and preventing complications.

  • Monitor blood sugar levels regularly
  • Follow prescribed medication schedules
  • Maintain a consistent eating schedule
  • Exercise regularly as recommended
  • Attend regular medical checkups

Mental Health

Recognizing Mental Health Issues

Understanding signs of mental health challenges and when to seek help.

  • Persistent sadness or anxiety lasting weeks
  • Loss of interest in activities once enjoyed
  • Significant changes in sleep or appetite
  • Difficulty concentrating or making decisions
  • Thoughts of self-harm or suicide
Mental Health Warning Signs

Stress Management Techniques

Simple, effective ways to manage stress and improve mental wellbeing.

  • Practice deep breathing exercises daily
  • Maintain regular physical activity
  • Connect with family and friends regularly
  • Establish healthy sleep routines
  • Seek professional help when needed
